# CADEX
**Consulting Advisor & Deal EXecution Framework**

A structured tool for presales, solutioning, and delivery teams to qualify deals, select engagement strategies, check proposals, and hand off cleanly to delivery.

## What It Does

- **Risk-scores** any consulting deal across 6 axes in under 30 minutes
- **Recommends** the right engagement strategy (6 strategies: Foundation-First through No-Bid)
- **Shapes** the deal with specific scope, commercial, and governance levers
- **Checks** proposals against a 30-point quality gate before submission
- **Generates** handoff artifacts for delivery teams
